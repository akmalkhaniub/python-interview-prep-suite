# Job Description: Lead AI Product Engineer

## Role Overview
**Location:** Islamabad / Hybrid
**Compensation:** Competitive Base Salary + Direct MRR Revenue Share
**Status:** High-leverage role focused on building commercial AI-driven SaaS and internal MarTech tools.

---

## What You Will Build
### 1. Commercial SaaS Development (B2B & B2C)
- Architect and scale secure **multi-tenant** environments.
- Implement strict **Firebase/Firestore security rules** for data isolation.
- Integrate **Stripe** for tiered subscriptions and usage-based (token) billing.

### 2. Internal MarTech & Content Pipelines
- Architect microservices to scrape context.
- Orchestrate **Claude API** (Anthropic) for content generation.
- Enforce strict JSON formatting and deploy to CMS/marketing channels.

---

## The Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js (SSR, performance optimization).
- **Backend:** Node.js & Python (FastAPI/Flask).
- **Database & Auth:** Firebase (Firestore, Auth, Security Rules).
- **AI:** Anthropic Claude API, Prompt Engineering, Context Window Management.
- **Tools:** Stripe integration, Web Scraping, CMS deployment.

---

## Requirements & Mindset
- **Autonomy:** Absolute ownership of architecture.
- **Marketing Mindset:** SEO-native software, A/B testing, automated outreach.
- **Speed:** Value speed of execution over endlessly polishing code.
- **Communication:** Translate AI capabilities into business value.

---

## Compensation Highlights
- **Direct Revenue Sharing:** Milestone-based percentage of Gross Revenue (MRR).
- **No "Hollywood Accounting":** Direct routing of profit share.
- **High Upside:** Income scales with the product.
