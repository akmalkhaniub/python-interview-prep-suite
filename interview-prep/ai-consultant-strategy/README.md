## 🎯 Key Focus Areas (Based on JD)
- **Business Value:** How to quantify the "Why" behind an AI project.
- **Discovery:** Structured methods for extracting requirements and identifying pain points.
- **Architecture:** High-level design that considers security, cost, and scalability.
- **Governance:** Building trust through ethical and compliant AI frameworks.
- **Presence:** Managing high-stakes conversations with non-technical executives.
