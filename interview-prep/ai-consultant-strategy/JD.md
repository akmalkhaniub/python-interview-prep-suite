# AI Consultant (Strategy & Implementation)

## Role Overview
**Role:** AI Consultant
**Location:** Remote
**Compensation:** $115,000 – $175,000 + Benefits
**Type:** Full-time

Advise organizations on adopting and deploying AI. Combine strategic framing with hands-on implementation guidance, helping clients move from initial interest to working systems that deliver real value.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Strategic Advisory:** Advise client leadership on AI strategy, use cases, and ROI.
- **Discovery Workshops:** Run workshops to identify high-value AI opportunities.
- **Solution Architecture:** Design high-level architectures for AI implementations.
- **Implementation Partner:** Partner with engineering teams on PoCs and pilots.
- **Governance & Risk:** Develop frameworks for AI adoption, ethics, and risk management.
- **Executive Communication:** Present recommendations to C-suite and executive audiences.

## Required Skills & Qualifications
- **Experience:** 5+ years in management consulting, tech strategy, or AI implementation.
- **Technical Knowledge:** Strong understanding of LLMs, Computer Vision, and ML.
- **Communication:** Excellent written/verbal skills with executive presence.
- **Delivery:** Experience scoping and delivering complex client engagements.
- **Education:** MBA, Computer Science, or equivalent experience preferred.

## Behavioral Traits
- Sharp analytical thinking and ability to structure ambiguity.
- Bias toward action and driving recommendations to outcomes.
- Comfort working asynchronously across time zones.
