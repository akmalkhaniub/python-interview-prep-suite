# 01: Video Pitch Script Template

Aim for **90 seconds**. This leaves room for natural pauses and ensures you don't exceed the 2-minute limit.

---

### Phase 1: The Hook (15 Seconds)
*Goal: Establish identity and seniority immediately.*

> "Hi, I’m **[Your Name]**, a Senior AI/ML Engineer specializing in building production-grade LLM applications. Over the last **[X]** years, I’ve focused on bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and scalable, real-world AI systems."

### Phase 2: The "Meat" - Technical Expertise (45 Seconds)
*Goal: Hit the keywords from the JD (RAG, Agents, MLOps).*

> "My core expertise lies in architecting **Advanced RAG pipelines** and **Agentic workflows**. For instance, in my recent work, I developed a system that [**Briefly describe a win: e.g., reduced hallucination by 30% or automated a complex document workflow using LangChain/AutoGPT patterns**]. 
> 
> I’m deeply proficient in the **Python/PyTorch** ecosystem and have a strong focus on the 'Ops' side—ensuring that models aren't just performing in notebooks, but are robust, monitored, and cost-optimized in production environments."

### Phase 3: The "Value Add" - Why This Startup? (20 Seconds)
*Goal: Show alignment with a US-based, fast-paced startup.*

> "I thrive in fast-paced startup environments where I can own the full lifecycle—from prompt engineering and model fine-tuning to deploying via robust CI/CD pipelines. I'm particularly excited about what you're building because [**mention one thing: e.g., your focus on intelligent agents / the specific industry**]."

### Phase 4: The Call to Action (10 Seconds)
*Goal: Professional closing.*

> "I’m looking forward to discussing how my background in LLM-powered systems can help drive your technical roadmap forward. Thanks for your time!"

---

## 💡 Quick Tips for Customization
- **Don't Read:** Use bullet points. Reading a script verbatim makes you look robotic.
- **The "So What?":** Don't just say "I know RAG." Say "I used RAG to solve [Problem] which resulted in [Outcome]."
- **Framework Focus:** If they mention TensorFlow/PyTorch specifically, swap the mention in Phase 2.
