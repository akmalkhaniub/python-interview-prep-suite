# AI Software Engineer - Cloud

**Location:** Remote (Work from Anywhere)
**Type:** Senior Contractor

Lead the development of cutting-edge software projects that integrate multi-language systems and AI engineering. Work independently on complex technical challenges, driving innovation across systems programming, backend development, and machine learning pipelines.

- **Task Design:** Create realistic programming tasks reflecting authentic engineering workflows.
- **Troubleshooting:** Diagnose and repair sophisticated bugs in diverse codebases.

- **Codebase Health:** Develop and maintain high-quality, secure, and scalable code.
- **AI Solutions:** Collaborate on designing, developing, and deploying AI-powered solutions.

## Required Skills & Qualifications
- **Systems Programming:** Expertise in C++, Java, Python, and Go.

- **ML Pipelines:** Proficiency in data preprocessing, model training, and deployment.
- **DevOps:** Strong understanding of CI/CD, automated testing, and Docker.
- **Independence:** Ability to work with minimal supervision on complex challenges.
