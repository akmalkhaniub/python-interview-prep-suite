# Developer Relations (Tether - QVAC AI)

## Role Overview
**Role:** Developer Relations / Developer Advocate
**Company:** Tether (Finance, Data, Power, Education)
**Product:** QVAC (Privacy-focused, local-first AI platform)
**Location:** Remote

Engage, educate, and inspire the developer community to drive adoption of Tether's technology, specifically the QVAC platform. Bridge the gap between external developers and internal product teams while creating high-quality technical content and advocating for the technology stack.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Community Building:** Nurture relationships, host AMAs/webinars, and synthesize developer feedback.
- **Technical Content:** Create tutorials, blog posts, sample apps, and documentation for SDKs/APIs.
- **Product Evangelism:** Speak at industry events, translate complex concepts into value propositions, and track adoption metrics.
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Partner with engineering, product, and marketing to align developer needs with product strategy.
- **Local AI Advocacy:** Champion the use of QVAC for privacy-focused, local-device AI applications.

## Required Skills & Qualifications
- **Software Dev:** 3+ years of professional experience (JS, TS, Node.js).
- **DevRel:** Previous experience as a Developer Advocate or Community Manager.
- **AI/ML Mastery:** Strong experience with **Llama.cpp** and **ggml** inference engines.
- **Content Creation:** Proven ability to create technical tutorials and reference implementations.
- **Communication:** Excellent public speaking and writing skills (English fluency).
- **Tools:** Experience with AI-assisted development workflows (Cursor, etc.).

## Preferred Qualifications
- 4–6 years of experience in DevRel or Software Development.
- Open-source contributions or maintaining public GitHub repos.
- Experience designing/promoting SDKs and developer tools.
- Understanding of DevRel success metrics (reach, engagement, adoption).
