# JD Reference: Senior Technical SEO & Frontend Engineer

> **Note:** Anonymized version of the job description. Company-identifying details removed.

---

## Role Summary

**Title:** Senior Technical SEO & Frontend Engineer  
**Role Type:** Hybrid Engineering & SEO Implementation  

This is not a pure marketing role, nor is it a generic frontend role. The ideal candidate can ship pages in code while understanding SEO deeply enough to diagnose and fix ranking, indexing, and site architecture issues. The core mission is to own the implementation of SEO-focused landing pages, build scalable systems for organic growth, and improve the technical SEO foundation.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Page Development:** Build high-quality landing pages for SEO at scale.
- **Calculator/Templates:** Build calculator pages and reusable page templates.
- **Technical SEO Auditing:** Audit and fix technical SEO issues across the site.
- **On-Page Optimization:** Improve metadata, canonicals, schema markup, internal linking, sitemap structure, and crawlability.
- **Performance & Rankings:** Help improve indexation, rankings, CTR, and Core Web Vitals.
- **System Scalability:** Work on scalable page architecture to launch many pages efficiently without creating thin or duplicate content.
- **Opportunity Identification:** Identify SEO opportunities and turn them into shipped pages (not just recommendations).
- **Collaboration & Speed:** Collaborate with founders and move quickly with a high degree of ownership.

---

## Requirements

- **2+ years** of work experience with **React.js**.
- Strong experience with **Next.js**, React, and modern frontend development.
- Strong practical experience with **Technical SEO**.
- Experience building SEO landing pages, **programmatic SEO (pSEO)** pages, or template-driven content systems.
- Experience implementing:
  - Metadata
  - Canonicals
  - Noindex logic
  - Schema markup
  - Sitemaps & robots.txt
  - Internal linking
  - Page speed improvements
- Ability to work independently and make smart product/SEO decisions.
- Strong experience using **Claude code** (or similar AI-assisted coding tools) is a **must**.
- Ability to explain what was built, why it was built, and the impact it had.
- Good English communication skills.

## Strong Bonus Points

- Experience working on a **SaaS product**.
- Experience building **calculator pages**.
- Experience improving **Core Web Vitals**.
- Experience with **large-scale landing page systems**.
- Experience in finance, investing, or data products.

---

## Role Fit

**Great Fit If You Are:**
- A developer who understands SEO (not just following tickets).
- Someone who likes ownership and shipping fast.
- Able to look at a site, find what's broken, prioritize, and fix it.
- A systems thinker focusing on scalability, not one-off pages.

**Not a Fit If You Are:**
- Mainly a content writer or SEO strategist who does not code.
- Mainly a designer.
- A generic frontend developer with no real SEO experience.
- Someone who only gives recommendations and needs others to execute them.
