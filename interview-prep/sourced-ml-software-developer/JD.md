# Machine Learning Software Developer (Remote)

## Role Overview
**Company:** Sourced (for a global leader in AI)
**Location:** Remote
**Payout:** $30 - $100 /hour
**Type:** Contract

Join one of our clients, a global leader in the Artificial Intelligence industry, as a skilled Machine Learning Engineer working on a contract basis. This role involves leveraging advanced technical skills to design, develop, and optimize state-of-the-art machine learning models. You will play a pivotal role in solving complex data challenges, collaborating closely with cross-functional stakeholders, and driving impactful results in a fully remote environment.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Develop and train ML models** using TensorFlow and Scikit-learn to address real-world business problems.
- **Conduct comprehensive data preprocessing**, including data cleaning, transformation, and feature engineering to ensure high-quality inputs.
- **Collaborate** with data scientists, data engineers, and business analysts to translate requirements into scalable ML solutions.
- **Continuously monitor, evaluate, and optimize** ML models to ensure performance and business objectives are met.
- **Analyze performance** and provide actionable insights to stakeholders.

## Required Skills & Qualifications
- **Advanced knowledge of ML concepts**: Supervised/unsupervised learning, neural networks, and deep learning.
- **Expertise in TensorFlow and Scikit-learn**: Experience in developing and deploying ML models.
- **Data Preprocessing**: Strong understanding of cleaning, transformation, and feature engineering.
- **Collaboration & Communication**: Experience working with cross-functional teams.
- **Analytical & Problem-Solving**: Ability to analyze complex data sets and identify trends/patterns.
