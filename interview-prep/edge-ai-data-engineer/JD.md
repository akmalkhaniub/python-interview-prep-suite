# Job Description: AI & Data Engineer

## Role Overview
**Company:** Edge (Global talent platform for medical/dental/insurance)
**Location:** Remote / Global
**Reporting to:** Manager, Data Platform (Direct visibility to CEO)
**Status:** Hybrid role at the intersection of Data Engineering, AI Systems, and Business Execution.

---

## What You'll Do
### 1. Data Engineering & Analytics (GA4 Specialist)
- Build and maintain **ETL/ELT pipelines** and warehouse models.
- **GA4 Mastery:** Own event schema design, custom dimensions, and GTM (including server-side).
- Pipe GA4 data into **BigQuery** and unify it with CRM/Product/Financial data.
- Partner with Marketing/Growth to define **attribution models** and funnels.

### 2. Agentic AI & Automation
- Design and build **Agentic AI systems** (LangGraph, LlamaIndex, **CrewAI**).
- Implement RAG pipelines, tool use, and memory in production.
- Automate business functions: **Outbound sales workflows**, talent pipeline optimization, and compliance.

### 3. Integrations & Systems
- Integrate **Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack**, and internal platforms using APIs and webhooks.
- Build reliable connectors and event-driven architectures.
- Monitor integrations and implement alerting/recovery mechanisms.

---

## Technical Requirements
- **Data:** Strong ETL/ELT, Data modeling, BigQuery (strongly preferred), SQL.
- **GA4:** Expert level (GTM, BigQuery export, reporting).
- **AI:** LangGraph, LlamaIndex, CrewAI, RAG, Tool use.
- **Dev:** Python, APIs, REST, Webhooks.
- **Cloud:** GCP (primary), AWS.
- **Nice to have:** dbt, Airflow/Dagster, Server-side GTM, Consent Mode v2, PowerBI.

---

## Mindset
- **High Ownership:** Direct impact on growth trajectory.
- **Bias for Action:** Delivering production systems, not just prototypes.
- **Business Focus:** Bridging the gap between technical code and business outcomes.
