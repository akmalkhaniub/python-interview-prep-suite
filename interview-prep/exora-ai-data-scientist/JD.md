# Job Description: Data Scientist Engineer @ Exora AI

## Role Overview
**Company:** Exora AI
**Status:** Focus on the full Machine Learning lifecycle, from data preprocessing to production deployment and monitoring.

---

## Key Responsibilities
- **Model Development:** Develop ML/DL models tailored to business needs.
- **Data Preprocessing:** Collect, clean, and handle anomalies (missing values, outliers).
- **Model Training:** Optimize hyperparameters, cross-validation, and regularization.
- **Evaluation:** Analyze outputs using relevant metrics and iterate on design.
- **Deployment:** Integrate models into production for real-time or batch inference.
- **Monitoring:** Track performance degradation and data drift; implement automated alerts.
- **Documentation:** Maintain version control and documentation for reproducibility.

---

## Technical Requirements
- **Education:** Bachelor’s, Master’s, or Ph.D. in CS, Engineering, Math, or Stats.
- **Programming:** Proficiency in **Python**, R, or Julia.
- **Frameworks:** **TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn**, Keras.
- **Data Tools:** Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn.
- **Expertise:** Supervised/Unsupervised learning, CNNs, RNNs.
- **Software Engineering:** Git, Agile methodologies, collaborative development.

---

## Soft Skills
- **Problem-Solving:** Ability to break down complex problems into manageable components.
- **Communication:** Clearly explain technical concepts to diverse audiences.
- **Teamwork:** Work effectively in cross-functional teams.
