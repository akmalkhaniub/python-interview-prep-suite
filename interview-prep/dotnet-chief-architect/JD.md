# JD Reference: .NET Chief Architect (Remote)

> **Note:** Anonymized version of the job description. Company-identifying details removed.

---

## Role Summary

**Title:** .NET Chief Architect  
**Type:** Remote, Full-Time  
**Compensation:** $100,000/year USD  
**Team:** Platform Development  
**Product Domain:** ERP for social benefit organizations (nonprofits, school boards, government agencies)

Key member of the Platform Development team responsible for technical/architectural specifications, code review guidelines, coding prototypes and complex requirements. Works closely with developers to mentor and provide feedback. Acts as the product technical expert across departments.

---

## Responsibilities

- Evaluate technical designs to ensure they fit product standards
- Evaluate and suggest new technologies and platforms for the product
- Define technical and coding standards
- Define and participate in code reviews
- Code complex solutions and prototypes
- Define and manage the product release process
- Document application architecture
- Deliver deep dives to solve complex problems
- Develop and execute ITDs (Integration Test Documents)
- Mentor and educate developers
- Interact with other teams as the platform technical expert

---

## Requirements

- Diploma in Computer Science or relevant field
- **10+ years** of experience in .NET development
- **5+ years** of experience in full-stack development
- Hands-on experience implementing **unit/integration tests** in both backend and frontend
- Proven track record of effectively interacting with **senior management**
- Proven track record of creating and managing strong **product-driven standards**
- Strong English communications (oral and written)
- Quality mindset and detail-oriented

---

## Nice to Have

- **DevOps** experience
- Experience with **ERP product development**
- Post-secondary degree or diploma in a technology-related field
- Previous experience in Remote jobs
