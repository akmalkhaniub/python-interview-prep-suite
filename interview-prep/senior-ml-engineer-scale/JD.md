# Senior Machine Learning Engineer

## Role Overview
**Role:** Senior Machine Learning Engineer
**Location:** Remote
**Compensation:** $150,000 – $250,000 + Benefits
**Type:** Full-time

Lead the design, training, and deployment of ML systems in production. This role combines deep ML expertise with strong software engineering, owning models from research through scaled inference.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Production Scale:** Design, train, and deploy ML models at scale.
- **Architecture:** Lead decisions for training, evaluation, and inference pipelines.
- **Observability:** Build evaluation frameworks and monitoring for model quality and drift.
- **Data Engineering:** Partner on training data pipelines and feature stores.
- **Leadership:** Mentor junior ML engineers and contribute to ML practice.
- **Innovation:** Stay current on research and bring proven techniques into production.

## Required Skills & Qualifications
- **Experience:** 5+ years in MLE with production ownership.
- **Technical:** Strong Python, deep experience with PyTorch or TensorFlow.
- **Scale:** Experience training and serving models at scale (DL, LLMs, or classical ML).
- **Infrastructure:** Hands-on with cloud ML (AWS SageMaker, GCP Vertex, Databricks).
- **Engineering:** Strong fundamentals in testing, code review, and system design.
- **Impact:** Track record of shipping systems that delivered measurable business value.

## Behavioral Traits
- Curiosity about model failure modes.
- Rigor in following guidelines and flagging ambiguity early.
- Strong written communication and documentation skills.
- Comfort with asynchronous work across time zones.
