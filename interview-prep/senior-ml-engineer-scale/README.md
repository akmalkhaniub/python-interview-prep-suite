## 🎯 Key Focus Areas (Based on JD)
- **Scalability:** Moving from a notebook to a system that handles millions of requests.
- **Rigor:** Unit testing for data and models, not just code.
- **Infrastructure:** Deep understanding of SageMaker, Vertex AI, or Databricks workflows.
- **Monitoring:** Beyond accuracy—detecting drift and ensuring long-term reliability.
- **Mentorship:** How to lead projects and guide junior engineers.
