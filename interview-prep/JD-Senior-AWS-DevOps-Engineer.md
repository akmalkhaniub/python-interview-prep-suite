# JD Reference: Senior AWS DevOps Engineer (Remote)

> **Note:** This is an anonymized version of the job description used to generate notebooks 28–37. Company-identifying details have been removed.

---

## Role Summary

**Title:** Senior AWS DevOps Engineer  
**Type:** Remote, Full-Time  
**Team:** Small, high-impact platform team  
**Focus:** Building and scaling cloud-native infrastructure on AWS

This is a hands-on role contributing to both design and implementation of infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and platform tooling. The engineer works closely with engineering teams to improve system reliability, scalability, and developer experience. A high level of ownership is expected across the full lifecycle — from architecture decisions to hands-on delivery and operational support.

---

## Responsibilities

- Design, build, and maintain **AWS infrastructure** — improving scalability, resilience, and cost efficiency across environments
- Develop and manage **Infrastructure-as-Code using Terraform** — ensuring modular, reusable, and consistent deployments
- Design and implement **CI/CD pipelines (GitLab preferred)** across application, data, and infrastructure layers
- Build and maintain **Kubernetes (EKS)** workloads — including deployments, scaling, and production troubleshooting
- Work across a wide range of **AWS services**:
  - **Compute:** EC2, ECS, EKS, Lambda
  - **Networking:** VPCs, subnets, security groups
  - **Event-driven:** API Gateway, EventBridge, SQS/SNS, Step Functions
- Manage and optimize **data services** such as RDS (PostgreSQL) and ClickHouse
- Automate infrastructure and environment setup using **Packer and Ansible** for AMI creation and configuration
- Implement and improve **observability** using CloudWatch, Grafana, Prometheus, and centralized logging tools (e.g., Splunk, Datadog, ELK)
- Collaborate with developers and data engineers to improve **deployment workflows**, system reliability, and platform usability
- Apply **security best practices** across infrastructure, including IAM, KMS, and Secrets Manager
- Support **incident response**, troubleshooting, and continuous improvement of production systems

---

## Requirements

- 5+ years of experience in DevOps, Cloud Engineering, or SRE roles
- Strong hands-on experience with **AWS** across compute, networking, and serverless services
- Proven experience building and maintaining **CI/CD pipelines** (GitLab preferred)
- Strong proficiency with **Terraform** and Infrastructure-as-Code practices
- Hands-on experience with **Kubernetes** in production environments (EKS preferred)
- Solid understanding of containerization and orchestration concepts
- Experience implementing **monitoring, logging, and alerting** in production systems
- Experience working with **centralized observability platforms** (e.g., Splunk, Datadog, ELK, or similar)
- Strong understanding of **system reliability, scalability**, and production operations
- Experience working across both **Linux and Windows** environments
- Hands-on experience building **AMIs using Packer** and configuration tools such as **Ansible**
- Scripting skills (e.g., **Bash, Python, or PowerShell**)
- Ability to design systems and implement them hands-on in production environments
- Strong **ownership mindset** with the ability to operate in a small, fast-moving team

---

## Nice to Have

- Experience scaling or operating Kubernetes clusters at a larger scale
- Exposure to **multi-account AWS environments** and cross-account IAM roles
- Experience with **serverless and event-driven architectures**
- Familiarity with **PostgreSQL (RDS)** and/or **ClickHouse**
- Exposure to **real-time or streaming systems**
- Experience with specialized pipelines such as **Unreal Engine workflows** or **Pixel Streaming infrastructure**
