# Full-Stack Developer (Web & Mobile)

## Role Overview
**Role:** Full-Stack Developer
**Compensation:** $2,000 – $6,000 /month (Paid in USD)
**Location:** Remote (US-based company)
**Type:** Full-time (Independent Contractor)

Responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining both front-end and back-end systems for web and mobile applications. The role involves integrating APIs, managing databases, and collaborating in a distributed environment.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Full-Stack Development:** Maintain systems for both web (React) and mobile (React Native).
- **UI/UX:** Build responsive, high-performance interfaces using React.js and React Native.
- **Backend:** Develop robust services using Node.js, Python, Ruby on Rails, or Java.
- **APIs:** Integrate RESTful APIs and microservices for seamless client-server operations.
- **Data Management:** Manage databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB) with secure handling.
- **Collaboration:** Work with DevOps and Product teams to deliver seamless features.
- **Maintenance:** Troubleshoot issues across the stack and stay updated with trends.

## Required Skills & Qualifications
- **Experience:** 2-3 years of professional full-stack experience.
- **Frontend:** Proficiency in JavaScript, React.js, React Native, HTML5, and CSS3.
- **Backend:** Expertise in Node.js, Python, Ruby, or Java.
- **Databases:** Strong proficiency with PostgreSQL, MySQL, or MongoDB.
- **Cloud/DevOps:** Familiarity with AWS/GCP/Azure, Docker, and CI/CD pipelines.
- **Security:** Knowledge of authentication, authorization, and data protection.
- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in CS/Engineering or equivalent practical experience.

## Details
- Independent contractor agreement paid 2x monthly.
- Desire for long-term commitment.
