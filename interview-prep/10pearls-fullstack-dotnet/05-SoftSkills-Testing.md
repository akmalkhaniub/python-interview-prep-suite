# 05: Soft Skills, Testing & Agile

10Pearls values "heart and soul" and a commitment to mentorship and quality.

## 1. Testing Methodologies
- **Unit Testing (xUnit/NUnit):** Testing individual functions in isolation. Use **Moq** for mocking dependencies.
- **Integration Testing:** Testing how components work together (e.g., API + Database).
- **End-to-End (E2E) Testing (Playwright/Cypress):** Testing the full user flow.

## 2. Mentorship & Leadership
- **Code Reviews:** Not just finding bugs, but teaching "why" a certain approach is better.
- **Knowledge Sharing:** Leading "Lunch and Learns" or writing documentation.
- **Empathy:** Helping junior developers overcome technical challenges without micro-managing.

## 3. Agile / Scrum
- **Daily Standup:** What I did, what I'll do, and blockers.
- **Sprint Planning:** Estimating tasks (Story Points) and defining the Sprint Goal.
- **Retrospective:** Reflecting on what went well and what can be improved.

## 4. The 10Pearls Culture
- **"Doing well, while doing good":** Be prepared to talk about how you value social impact and work-life balance.
- **Innovation:** Showing that you stay updated with the latest tech (like .NET 8, AI integration, etc.).

## 5. Potential "Soft Skill" Interview Questions
1. **Describe a time you mentored a junior developer who was struggling.**
   - *Answer:* Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result). Focus on how you identified the gap, provided resources, and the positive outcome for the developer.
2. **How do you handle a conflict with a Project Manager regarding a deadline?**
   - *Answer:* Communicate early. Provide data-backed reasons why a deadline might be missed. Suggest "scope cutting" or "MVP approach" to meet the business needs without compromising quality.
3. **What is your approach to Code Reviews?**
   - *Answer:* I look for readability, performance, and adherence to "Clean Architecture". I phrase my comments as suggestions ("Have you considered...?") rather than demands.
4. **Why do you want to work at 10Pearls?**
   - *Answer:* Mention their award-winning culture, focus on innovation, and their commitment to both enterprise scale and startup agility.
