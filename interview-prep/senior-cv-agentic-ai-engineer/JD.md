# Senior Data Scientist (Computer Vision & Agentic AI)

## Role Overview
**Role:** Senior Data Scientist (Specializing in Computer Vision & Agentic AI)
**Experience:** 6–8 Years (B.Tech) / 4-6 Years (M.Tech)

Lead the design, development, and deployment of advanced vision-based AI systems. Deep expertise in traditional computer vision, feature engineering, and modern deep learning techniques is required, along with hands-on experience in building Agentic AI systems for intelligent decision-making.

## Key Responsibilities
- **End-to-End CV Pipelines:** Design and develop scalable, production-grade vision solutions.
- **Traditional Computer Vision:** Optimize algorithms for image processing, feature extraction, geometric transformations, tracking, and calibration.
- **Advanced Feature Engineering:** Extract meaningful visual features (texture, shape, color, spatial) and combine handcrafted features with deep learning embeddings.
- **Deep Learning:** Build and deploy models for Object Detection, Instance Segmentation, and Semantic Segmentation.
- **Agentic AI:** Develop systems that enable autonomous reasoning, orchestrate multi-step workflows, and integrate with external tools/APIs (LangChain, AutoGen).
- **Optimization:** Focus on performance, scalability, and edge deployment (ONNX).
- **Leadership:** Mentor junior members and contribute to architectural decisions.

## Required Skills & Qualifications
- **Core Technical:** OpenCV, PyTorch, TensorFlow, ONNX.
- **Traditional CV:** Image filtering, edge detection, morphology, feature matching, tracking.
- **Deep Learning Vision:** Object Detection, Semantic/Instance Segmentation.
- **Agentic AI:** Designing Agent-based systems using LLMs, familiarity with Tool usage, Planning, Memory, and frameworks like LangChain or AutoGen.
- **Multimodal:** Ability to combine vision models with LLMs for intelligent workflows.

## Preferred Qualifications
- **VLMs:** Experience with Vision-Language Models and multimodal systems.
- **MLOps:** Knowledge of MLflow, Azure ML, etc.
- **Edge AI:** Experience in real-time or edge AI systems.
- **Domains:** Automotive, Insurance, Retail, Manufacturing, or Surveillance.
