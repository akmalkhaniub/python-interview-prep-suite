# JD Reference: Full Stack Developer (React & Node.js)

> **Note:** Anonymized version of the job description. Company-identifying details removed.

---

## Role Summary

**Title:** Full Stack Developer (React & Node.js)  
**Location:** Karachi (On-site/Hybrid implied by specific location)  
**Experience Level:** Mid-to-Senior (3+ years hands-on experience)  

This role requires an independent Full Stack Engineer to own and deliver features across the entire technology stack. The engineer will develop web applications using React.js and Node.js, working closely with product managers and designers. The ideal candidate can manage tasks with minimal supervision, actively participates in code reviews, and has a strong problem-solving mindset.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Frontend:** Develop, maintain, and enhance web applications using **React.js**.
- **Backend:** Build backend services using **Node.js** with **Express.js** or **NestJS**.
- **APIs:** Design, build, and integrate **RESTful APIs** to connect frontend and backend efficiently.
- **Databases:** Work extensively with relational (**PostgreSQL, MySQL**) and non-relational (**MongoDB**) databases to design schema and manage data.
- **Product Delivery:** Translate product specifications and business requirements into technical solutions.
- **Ownership:** Take ownership of features from initial design through development, testing, deployment, and maintenance.
- **Code Quality:** Write clean, scalable, well-documented code following best practices.
- **Performance:** Debug, optimize, and enhance application performance for a smooth user experience.
- **Collaboration:** Collaborate closely with designers, product managers, and other engineers.
- **Code Reviews:** Participate actively in code reviews and technical discussions.
- **Self-Management:** Manage workload effectively to meet deadlines with minimal supervision.
- **Version Control:** Utilize **Git** to manage code and collaborate.
- **CI/CD:** Contribute to continuous integration and continuous deployment pipelines.
- **Continuous Learning:** Stay updated with emerging technologies and best practices.

---

## Requirements

- **3+ years** of hands-on experience in full stack development.
- Strong expertise in **JavaScript frameworks** (React.js, Node.js).
- Proficiency in **Express.js** or **NestJS**.
- Strong database management skills (relational and non-relational).
- Ability to independently own and deliver features.
- Strong problem-solving skills and a proactive learning attitude.

## Nice to Have

- Familiarity with **Cloud Services**.
