# AI Engineer (Production & Product)

## Role Overview
**Role:** AI Engineer
**Location:** Remote
**Compensation:** $130,000 – $220,000 + Benefits
**Type:** Full-time

Design, build, and ship AI-powered features and systems. Work at the intersection of machine learning, software engineering, and product—turning model capabilities into real product value.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Model Deployment:** Build, fine-tune, and deploy AI/ML models in production.
- **LLM Integration:** Integrate large language models (LLMs) into product features.
- **Evaluation:** Develop frameworks to measure model performance and quality.
- **System Design:** Partner with ML researchers and software engineers on architecture.
- **Iteration:** Monitor performance and iterate on prompts, fine-tuning, and architectures.
- **Infrastructure:** Maintain reliable, scalable inference infrastructure.

## Required Skills & Qualifications
- **Experience:** 3+ years building/deploying ML systems in production.
- **Technical:** Strong Python, familiarity with PyTorch/TensorFlow.
- **LLM Mastery:** Hands-on with OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google APIs.
- **Tools:** Working knowledge of Vector Databases, RAG, and Prompt Engineering.
- **Cloud:** Experience with AWS SageMaker, GCP Vertex AI, etc.
- **Fundamentals:** Solid software engineering principles.

## Behavioral Traits
- Curiosity about how models behave and why they fail.
- Rigor in following guidelines and flagging ambiguity early.
- Strong documentation and asynchronous communication skills.
