# Senior Full Stack / Applied AI Engineer

## Role Overview
**Role:** Senior Full Stack / Applied AI Engineer
**Location:** Remote (Smart Working)
**Type:** Full-time, Autonomous Product Role

Join a small, high-impact team at the intersection of product engineering and AI-driven solutions. This is not a traditional full-stack role; it requires high autonomy, product ownership from idea to production, and the ability to build AI-enabled features in a fast-paced, low-process environment.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Ownership:** Take features from idea to production across the entire stack.
- **AI Integration:** Rapidly prototype and ship AI-enabled solutions and LLM workflows.
- **Architecture:** Design scalable, maintainable, and secure system architectures.
- **Product Thinking:** Translate ambiguous stakeholder needs into structured user flows and solutions.
- **Execution:** End-to-end delivery of features, including UX improvements and data flows.
- **Continuous Improvement:** Scale existing Python apps and elevate engineering practices (SDLC, testing).

## Required Skills & Qualifications
- **Python Mastery:** 5+ years in production environments.
- **System Design:** 5+ years experience with architecture trade-offs and scalability.
- **Frontend Expertise:** 3+ years with React and modern JavaScript/HTML.
- **AI/ML Integration:** 3+ years integrating APIs (AI or data-driven) and AI-assisted dev tools.
- **Product Ownership:** 3+ years owning products/features end-to-end.
- **UX/Usability:** 2+ years designing user journeys and flows.
- **Data Engineering:** 2+ years handling data pipelines or ETL processes.

## Behavioral Traits
- High ownership mindset with the ability to operate independently.
- Ability to bring structure to ambiguous, low-structure environments.
- Proactive communicator capable of translating business needs to technical solutions.
- Balance between rapid delivery and long-term engineering quality.
