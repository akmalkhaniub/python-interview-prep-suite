# Job Description: Full Stack Developer (AI-Powered Platform)

## About the Company
We are an AI-powered care management platform purpose-built for home-care agencies. We enable regulated providers to streamline rotas, payroll, invoicing, mileage, and care planning through natural-language commands and our AI operations assistant. Our customers are scaling care agencies that depend on us to reduce administrative overhead while maintaining the highest standards of compliance and client safety.

## The Role
We're hiring a Full Stack Developer to own features end-to-end across our platform. You will deliver impactful work across scheduling, payroll, AI tooling, and infrastructure, partnering closely with care-agency operators who pilot new capabilities the same week they ship. This is a high-ownership role for an engineer who thrives on shipping production software in a regulated, mission-critical domain.

## Tech Stack
- **AI**: MCP (Model Context Protocol), LangChain, Pinecone, Anthropic, OpenAI
- **Frontend**: Next.js (App Router), React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Flutter
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express, TypeScript, PostgreSQL
- **Cloud & Infrastructure**: AWS (ECS, RDS, S3, CloudFront, Cognito, SES), Firebase, Docker
- **Testing**: Vitest, Playwright, real-database integration tests

## Requirements
- Strong MCP (Model Context Protocol) experience — building servers, tools, and agent integrations
- 3+ years shipping production TypeScript across React/Next.js and Node.js
- Strong PostgreSQL fundamentals, including migrations, indexing strategies, and query optimisation
- Hands-on AWS experience with ECS, RDS, S3, CloudFront, IAM, and VPC
- Proficiency with Docker, including multi-stage builds and local development via Docker Compose
- Testing-first mindset covering unit, integration, and end-to-end testing
- Git fluency, including rebasing, bisect, and clean, reviewable pull requests
- Demonstrable attention to correctness in financial or healthcare contexts

## Bonus
- Experience in healthcare or other compliance-heavy domains (CQC, HIPAA, NHS)
- Advanced LLM and agent development: tool-use, streaming, evaluations
- Infrastructure-as-code experience with Terraform or AWS CDK
- Container orchestration (ECS, Kubernetes)
