# Member of Technical Staff (AI Engineering)

## Role Overview
**Role:** Member of Technical Staff (AI Engineering)
**Compensation:** $200,000 – $400,000 (USD equivalent)
**Location:** Remote
**Type:** Senior Individual Contributor (Frontier AI)

A leadership-level IC role at the frontier of AI development. Combine research-grade rigor with production engineering to build the systems that turn cutting-edge AI into real-world products. Lead projects across model architecture, alignment, and infrastructure.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Novel Systems:** Design and build novel AI systems (training, evaluation, inference).
- **Project Leadership:** Lead research-engineering projects in model architecture, alignment, and reliability.
- **Infrastructure:** Build and maintain large-scale training and inference infrastructure.
- **Evaluation:** Develop frameworks for assessing novel AI capabilities.
- **Thought Leadership:** Publish/present technical work and set technical direction.
- **Mentorship:** Mentor other AI engineers and foster a culture of excellence.

## Required Skills & Qualifications
- **Experience:** 6+ years in ML research or AI engineering at significant scale.
- **Expertise:** Deep knowledge of modern deep learning and transformer architectures.
- **Technical Mastery:** Strong PyTorch skills and distributed training experience.
- **Track Record:** Proven ability to ship high-impact AI systems.
- **Communication:** Excellent technical documentation and presentation skills.
- **Education:** PhD in CS/ML or equivalent high-level industry experience.

## Behavioral Traits
- Deep curiosity about model behavior and failure modes.
- Rigor in following guidelines and addressing ambiguity.
- Comfort working asynchronously across global time zones.
- Ability to bridge the gap between abstract research and concrete production.
